STU 210 Reflection

A reflection on learning, growth, and building this portfolio.

This website was created as part of STU 210 to demonstrate personal, academic, and professional growth throughout the semester. Rather than focusing solely on appearance, I aimed to design a site that reflects how I approach problem solving, organization, and long-term planning as a pre-engineering student.

Building this portfolio required balancing creativity with structure, while also managing the workload of engineering courses. The result is a project that represents not only my technical skills, but also my ability to adapt, learn, and iterate under time constraints.

Looking Ahead

As I move into the next semester, I am genuinely excited about the opportunities ahead. Continuing my engineering coursework means that the ideas I have been working on can finally become more complete, more complex, and more meaningful.

STU 210 has been a pleasure to be part of. This class gave me the space to reflect, build, and experiment while balancing the demands of engineering courses. I am extremely grateful for my time at Pima Community College and for the opportunities it has provided me.

I hope to continue taking advantage of future opportunities—academically, professionally, and personally—as I prepare to transfer and continue my education.

I also quickly learned that building a website from scratch in a short time frame—while taking engineering courses—is far more challenging than I initially expected. That challenge became part of the learning experience and reinforced the importance of planning, iteration, and persistence.

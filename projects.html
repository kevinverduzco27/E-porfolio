Projects

Conceptual, in-progress, and exploratory robotics projects that reflect my interests in engineering, systems design, and problem solving.

Smart Wildlife Deterrent Turret

A non-lethal, sensor-based deterrent system designed to discourage wildlife from entering dangerous areas near roadways.

The system would use motion detection and directional feedback (sound, light, or water) to redirect animals away from traffic.

Status: Concept & Design

Autonomous Car Cleaning Robot

A robotic system designed to automate exterior vehicle cleaning using controlled movement, brushes, and water delivery.

The goal is to explore mechanical design, motion planning, and efficiency while reducing manual labor.

Status: Concept

BB-8 Inspired Mobility Robot

A recreation of the BB-8 rolling robot platform with added mechanical attachment capability for experimental mobility assistance.

This project focuses on balance, internal drive systems, and unconventional locomotion design.

Status: Concept & Research

Laptop Guard Robot

A small desktop robot designed to monitor proximity and alert users when someone approaches unattended equipment.

The robot features a playful design, visual expressions, and audible alerts to deter theft without physical intervention.

Status: Concept

Automated Laundry Folding Robot

A robotic assistant intended to automate the repetitive task of folding clothing using mechanical arms and pattern recognition.

This project explores kinematics, precision control, and task automation in a domestic setting.

Status: Concept

VR-Controlled Combat Robot Platform

A remote-controlled robot system operated through virtual reality, inspired by competitive robotics and entertainment platforms.

Designed to keep participants safe while allowing immersive control, physics-based interaction, and real-world feedback.

Status: Concept

WALL·E Inspired Autonomous Robot

A robotics build inspired by WALL·E, based on existing schematics and mechanical designs with plans for full 3D printing.

The long-term goal is to integrate autonomy, expressive animation, and intelligent behavior for independent movement.

Status: Design & Planning
